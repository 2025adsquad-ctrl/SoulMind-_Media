Project Overview
Soulmind Media is the first wellness marketing agency specialized in mental health and holistic wellbeing services. Our goal is to bridge the gap between wellness providers and their target audiences by creating meaningful, empathetic, and impactful marketing strategies that resonate with people’s real needs. What makes us unique is our ability to combine psychology insight with creative marketing expertise, allowing us to craft authentic stories, build trust-driven campaigns, and help wellness brands connect deeply with the communities they serve.
 
Objectives
• Raise awareness about mental health and holistic wellbeing in Egypt.
• Help wellness and psychology service providers build strong, trusted brands.
• Design creative marketing campaigns that focus on empathy, trust, and value.
• Provide cost-effective solutions tailored to small and medium wellness businesses.
• Position Soulmind Media as the go-to partner for wellness-focused marketing.
 
Scope
Soulmind Media will specialize in:
• Digital Marketing Campaigns for wellness services.
• Social Media Strategy & Management with focus on storytelling and community building.
• Content Creation (articles, videos, podcasts) tailored to wellness audiences.
• Brand Identity Development for psychologists, therapists, coaches, and retreats.
• Awareness Campaigns for holistic wellbeing initiatives.
 
Plan
Phase 1: Research & Positioning
• Study wellness market in Egypt and identify key audience pain points.
• Map competitors and define Soulmind’s unique value proposition.
Phase 2: Strategy Development
• Build service packages tailored to small wellness providers.
• Define communication tone (empathetic, professional, trustworthy).
Phase 3: Launch & Content Creation
• Develop pilot campaigns for early clients.
• Build a strong online presence for Soulmind Media.
Phase 4: Growth & Partnerships
• Partner with wellness centers, retreats, and clinics.
• Expand services with workshops, webinars, and community events.
